ATTENTION!
Dear students, you can apply for visa or registration extension online in advance.
Apply for a visa extension before 30 days of its expiration.
If you live in a dormitory at Cosmonavtov st., then for questions about visa extension you need to write to
mk.belova@mpgu.su
. To extend your registration you need to contact
mo.eliseev@mpgu.su
If you live in a dormitory on Prospect Vernadskogo, then for questions about visa extension and registration you need to write to
kv.kluchnikov@mpgu.su
If you live not in a dormitory, but in an apartment, then for questions about extending your registration you need to write to this email —
mk.belova@mpgu.su
Please, write the following information in your email:
Dormitory number or apartment address
Your name in Russian
Faculty
Year of your studies
Mobile telephone number
After document submission, Passport and Visa Department of MPGU International Relations Office checks your documents, then sends you payment details (state fee for a new visa) and gives you some instructions when you should bring a receipt/a passport/a registration.
If you require a consultation, send an email to
ta.kolpakova@mpgu.su
Please, send us copies in PDF/DOC, DOCX (Visa application letter).
