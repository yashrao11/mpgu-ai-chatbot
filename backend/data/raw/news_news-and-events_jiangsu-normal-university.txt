Visit of Jiangsu Normal University to MPGU
On February 21, 2024, a delegation from Jiangsu Normal University (JNU) visited Moscow Pedagogical State University. The Chinese partners, led by Rector Zhou Zhuguang, were warmly welcomed by first vice-rector Victor Dronov.
The JNU has established friendly relations with more than 81 universities from 35 countries, being one of the first Chinese universities authorized to accept foreign students. Both universities have been collaborating for about 20 years. The long-term partnership and the results achieved are evidence of mutual interest and desire to continue training qualified personnel.
A foreign delegation arrived at Moscow Pedagogical State University to discuss the terms of extending the existing agreement between universities, as well as discuss cooperation in the field of mathematics education. The parties discussed the prospects for creating a joint educational program.
Both sides expressed a desire to develop promising areas of interaction in the field of academic mobility, training of teaching staff, and development of educational programs.
