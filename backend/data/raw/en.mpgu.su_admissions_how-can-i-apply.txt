The contract is finalised within 5 working days. The completed contract, receipt and detailed instructions will be sent to your e-mail address (the letter will come from Tatyana Guldyasheva: ti.guldyasheva@mpgu.su). For a contractual transfer: It is necessary to write to the email of Tatyana Guldyasheva (ti.guldyasheva@mpgu.su), indicate where you want to enroll (course, faculty, field of study, form of study) and attach the following documents: passport, visa and notarized translation of the passport; certificate of the period of study (with a list of completed disciplines); extract from the record book; letter of consent for transfer from the university where you study (if you transfer from another university); certificate that you are a student. Next, wait for the documents to be filled in by mail. For readmission on a contractual basis It is necessary to write to the email of Tatyana Guldyasheva (ti.guldyasheva@mpgu.su), indicate where you want to enroll (course, faculty, field of study, form of study) and attach a passport, a visa and a notarized translation of the passport. Next, wait for the documents to be filled in by mail. ATTENTION: READMISSION THROUGH THE STATE LINE IS IMPOSSIBLE. If you are studying under the quota of the Ministry of Education of the Russian Federation (Rossotrudnichestvo programme), then the possibility of transferring while maintaining the quota is considered on an individual basis. It is necessary to write to the email of Yulia Aleksandrovna Lyulchak (yua.lyulchak@mpgu.su) and attach a set of documents: application for transfer (in free form); a letter of consent for the transfer from the Embassy of your country; letter of consent for transfer from the university where you study (if you transfer from another university); passport, visa and notarized translation of the passport; extract from the record book; certificate of the period of study (with a list of completed disciplines); a certificate stating that you are a student. Next, wait for the documents to be filled in by mail.
